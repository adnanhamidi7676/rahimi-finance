# Rahimi Finance — Staff Guide

A short, non-technical guide to everyday tasks. You sign in with the email and
password an administrator gave you.

---

## Signing in

1. Open the app (or tap its icon if you installed it).
2. Enter your **email** and **password** → **Sign in**.
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard**.

To move around, use the **left menu** on a computer, or the **bottom bar** on a
phone: Dashboard · Projects · Cash Book · Employees (· Admin for administrators).

---

## How to add a project

1. Go to **Projects**.
2. Tap **Add Project**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Project name** (e.g. *Poultry Management System*)
   - **Client** (pick from the list — add new clients under *Admin → Clients*)
   - **Date**
   - **Total amount** and choose the currency (**USD** or **AFN**)
   - **Employee** — the developer. Picking one fills in their default share %.
   - **Employee %** (default 10%) and **Office %** (default 5%)
   - **Amount paid** so far
4. Tap **Add project**.

The **Employee Share, Office Share, Manager Share** and **Remaining Balance** are
calculated automatically — you never type them. They appear in the grey columns.

> **Edit / delete:** tap the **⋮** menu on a project row (or card on mobile).

**Filters & search** at the top let you narrow by employee, client, paid status,
or date range. The **Totals** row at the bottom sums the visible projects per
currency.

---

## How to add a cash entry (Cash Book)

1. Go to **Cash Book**.
2. Tap **Add Entry**.
3. Choose the direction:
   - **Cash In (Debit)** — money received
   - **Cash Out (Credit)** — money paid out
4. Enter the **amount** and currency (default **AFN**).
5. Type the **description** — Pashto/Dari text is fine; it aligns automatically.
6. Pick the **date** → **Save entry**.

The **Balance** column is a running total and updates itself. You don't enter it.

---

## Live updates

The app is shared and live. When a colleague adds or edits something, it appears
on your screen within a second — no need to refresh. A small **Live** indicator
shows the realtime connection is active.

---

## Administrators only

**Admin → Users**

- **Add user:** enter their name, email, a temporary password, and a role, then
  **Create user**. Share the email + temporary password with them.
- **Change a role:** use the dropdown next to a user.
- **Activate / deactivate:** tap the status badge. Deactivated users can't sign
  in. (You can't deactivate yourself.)

**Roles**

- **Admin** — full access, plus user management.
- **Manager** — can add/edit all finance data, but not manage users.
- **Viewer** — read-only.

**Admin → Clients** — add, rename, or remove the clients that appear in the
project form.

---

## Installing the app

- **Phone (Android, Chrome):** menu **⋮ → Install app**.
- **iPhone (Safari):** **Share → Add to Home Screen**.
- **Computer (Chrome/Edge):** the **Install** icon in the address bar.

It then behaves like a normal app with its own icon.
